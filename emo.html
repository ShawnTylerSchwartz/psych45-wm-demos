We strongly suggest using Chrome as your browser.

You can try the demo in a different browser, but it may not work properly.

In this experiment, you will see a series of words displayed on the screen.

After the list ends, your job is to remember as many words from the list as possible.

Once the last word is shown, a text box will appear for you to write down the words you remember.
